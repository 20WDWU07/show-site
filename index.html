Web & UX Module1

Module 1 is all about UX (User Experience) Design and runs for 10 weeks. UX research is the key to good design. Students strategize, research, design, develop and test website. Users are involved at every stage. CSS style guides are prepared to follow best practices of Yoobee and web industry. Developers choose CSS Methodologies likeBEM OOCSS and use SASS as a CSS pre-processor to organize styling faster with partials and mixins. GitHub is used for version control. Implications to the Treaty of Waitangi are considered.

In this course we learned more about HTML, CSS and JavaScript techniques. The intention is to implement new features to keep on challenging ourselves as we dive deep into the web development world. We as students want to invest time and effort into our projects in order to gain experience and build a great portfolio.

TIPS

  • Grind coffee into powder
  • Grinded coffee powder gives freshest flavour
  • Place grinds in coffee basket
  • Single 30ml double 60ml

Grinds

Store bought is more oxidized and loses flavour and is typically more sweet. When choosing coffee beans it is important to choose ethically sourced coffee as there is a high demand for coffee. Rainforests can be cleared for coffee plantations and look for stickers such as fair trade, rainforest alliance, ethically sourced labels.

Tamper

Squash grinds in the basket and using the tamper tap it hard enough and wiggle it around for a smooth polish

Coffee extraction

Place the basket in espresso machine and lock it in by turning it This runs water through the coffee grind to extract coffee Wait till the coffee turns a blonde colour and stop to avoid burning the coffee

Milk

For foam place the wand higher in the jug this pushes air into the milk and creates bubbles. Place on 45 degree angle and aim for a consistent swirl until the jug is too hot to touch to avoid burning the milk

Maintenance

After use flush out coffee grinds and clean the steaming wand For a deep clean most machines have a cleaning cycle or you can run baking soda or vinegar through the machine.

Variations of coffee

There's a lot of variations of coffee including cappuccinos, americanos, mochas, flat whites, and lattes These are all determined by the amount of coffee, milk and foam.
